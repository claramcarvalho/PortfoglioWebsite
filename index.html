My name is Clara, and welcome to my digital space! I'm a dedicated and passionate developer who loves solving practical problems and bringing digital solutions to processes, projects and people.

I took courses and training and became an internal reference on the subject of databases and data analysis.

I therefore decided to pursue my passion for programming and my digital aptitude, and make a career change towards systems and software development.
